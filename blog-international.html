Top 7 Low Budget Countries

When comparing the cost of living and travel expenses to India, several countries may offer relatively cheaper options for travelers. However, it's essential to consider various factors such as accommodation, transportation, food, activities, and exchange rates. Here are some countries that are generally considered to be cheaper for travelers compared to India:

Nepal

Nepal is a neighboring country to India and offers budget-friendly travel options, especially for trekking and adventure activities in the Himalayas. Accommodation, food, and transportation are generally more affordable compared to many other countries.

Sri Lanka

Known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and vibrant culture, Sri Lanka offers affordable travel experiences. Accommodation, local transportation, and food are relatively cheap, making it an attractive destination for budget travelers.

Vietnam

It is famous for its beautiful landscapes, bustling cities, and delicious cuisine. Travelers can explore attractions like Ha Long Bay, Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, and Hoi An at affordable prices. Accommodation, street food, and local transportation are reasonably priced.

Thailand

It is a popular destination known for its tropical beaches, bustling markets, and vibrant nightlife. While some tourist areas can be more expensive, Thailand still offers budget-friendly options for accommodation, street food, and transportation, especially in less touristy areas.

Indonesia

With its diverse landscapes, including stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and volcanic mountains, Indonesia offers affordable travel experiences. Places like Bali, Yogyakarta, and Lombok provide budget-friendly accommodation, food, and activities.

Philippines

The Philippines boasts stunning islands, crystal-clear waters, and rich marine life, making it a paradise for beach lovers and adventurers. Accommodation, food, and activities are relatively affordable, especially outside of major tourist areas.

Malaysia

While some parts of Malaysia, such as Kuala Lumpur, can be relatively expensive, other areas like Penang and Langkawi offer more affordable options for travelers. Accommodation, street food, and local transportation are generally cheaper compared to many Western countries.
