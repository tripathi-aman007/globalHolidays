Top 10 Indian Pilgrimages

Kedarnath

Located in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand, Kedarnath is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas and one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Badrinath

Nestled in the Garhwal Himalayas of Uttarakhand, Badrinath is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites and is dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Varanasi (Kashi)

Situated on the banks of the Ganges River, Varanasi is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world and a major pilgrimage site for Hindus.

Haridwar

Located in the state of Uttarakhand, Haridwar is considered one of the seven holiest places in Hinduism. It's known for its ghats and the famous Ganga Aarti ceremony.

Rishikesh

Another holy city in Uttarakhand, Rishikesh is renowned as the "Yoga Capital of the World" and is frequented by spiritual seekers and pilgrims alike.

Amarnath

The Amarnath Cave, located in the state of Jammu and Kashmir, is one of the most important Hindu pilgrimage sites. It houses an ice stalagmite formed naturally, which is believed to represent Lord Shiva.

Tirupati

Home to the renowned Venkateswara Temple, Tirupati in Andhra Pradesh is one of the wealthiest and most visited pilgrimage destinations in the world.

Vaishno Devi

Situated in the Trikuta Mountains of Jammu and Kashmir, the Vaishno Devi Temple is dedicated to the Hindu Goddess Vaishno Devi and attracts millions of devotees every year.

Golden Temple

Located in Amritsar, Punjab, the Golden Temple is the holiest shrine in Sikhism. It's known for its stunning architecture and the sacred Amrit Sarovar (pool of nectar) surrounding it.

Puri Jagannath Temple

Situated in the state of Odisha, the Jagannath Temple in Puri is dedicated to Lord Jagannath (an incarnation of Lord Vishnu), and it's one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites for Hindus.
